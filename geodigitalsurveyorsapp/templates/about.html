Who We Are

Geo Digital Surveyors is a registered land surveying firm committed to excellence, innovation, and reliability in every project. With decades of combined experience in land administration, mapping, and planning, we serve real estate developers, local governments, and private landowners across Kenya.

  • Certified professionals & licensed surveyors
  • Modern equipment & GIS-backed tools
  • Transparent, client-centered processes

Meet the Founder

Ronny, the founder and lead surveyor of Geo Digital Surveyors, is a qualified and licensed professional with deep roots in spatial planning and land development. With years of experience working on both private and public projects, he brings unmatched attention to detail, legal knowledge, and passion to every client engagement.

His mission is simple: “to deliver trusted land solutions with integrity, accuracy, and value.”

Whether you're processing a title deed, mapping a new estate, or resolving land boundary disputes – you're in capable hands.
