Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 2025

At Geo Digital Surveyors, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy outlines the type of personal data we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have regarding your information.

1. Information We Collect

2. How We Use Your Information

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties unless:

  • It’s required by law or legal process.

4. Data Security

We implement technical and organizational measures to safeguard your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

5. Your Rights

You may request to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time by contacting us via the details provided on our contact page.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised date.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our Privacy Policy, feel free to reach out through our Contact Page.
